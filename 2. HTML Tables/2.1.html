X1 - Emergency: Quantum Leap

K-Pop Boyband X1

X1 is the name of a k-pop group formed through Korean reality TV show Produce X 101. It featured 101 trainees from 47 different entertainment companies and many independent trainees. The public voted on 11 members to form the group X1. Their first album, Emergency: Quantum Leap, was released on August 27, 2019.
