Google Pixelbook

Valued at $1300, the Google Pixelbook runs on Chrome OS, a fast OS that serves Google's line of Chromebooks and Pixelbooks. It operates solely with the web browser Googel Chrome. The Google Pixelbook model boasts an incredible HD display, light build, and a number of auxiliary inputs/outputs. It has no cooling system, and only two peripheral ports.
