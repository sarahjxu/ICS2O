Macbook Air 2018

Valued at $1200, Macbook Air 2018 runs on macOS, arguably the most visually appealing operating software of 20XX. This model features the standard RAM, great HD, a moderate CPU and GPU, a thin and light build, improved audio from the last version, as well as a sleek design. The only downsides are the loud cooling system and limited peripheral ports.
